About Us

The kitchen team at Outback restaurant is made up of chefs and assistants covering many nationalities. They all bring a unique contribution. Some have been with the company for many years; others are more recent arrivals to this eclectic mix dedicated to great cooking led by the head chef and sous chefs.If you’ve never been to our restaurant before, you should know that it's a large dining room that can seat many people. With that sort of space, we have the capacity to host a range of events and special occasions.

To top it all off, The Outback Restaurant also has a full bar and a bakery. Whether you want to stop in for an after work drink or a late night pastry, you’re sure to find what you’re looking for and a whole lot more.

At The Outback Restaurant, we want to get to know you and your friends in the Binghamton, NY area. Hopefully you’ll come in and get to know us while enjoying our delicious food options. From beer to burgers, cheesecake to chops, we’re serving up what you're craving. Stop by our great Greek and American food diner in Binghamton, NY, tonight.
